Project Owners

Future home owners need bids on their homes. They come to the Internet Bid Room and upload the drawings for their new home in Adobe Acrobat PDF format. Then builders and/or subs who are looking for projects to build are notified that there is a new project to bid on. They can then view the project and bid right there. The project owner can receive the bids and see advertising for the builder and/or subs that are placed in their profiles. Bids can be accepted or rejected. Builders/Subs are then notified of their status on their bids or meetings can be negotiated with each other.
